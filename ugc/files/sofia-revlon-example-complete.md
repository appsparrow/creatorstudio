## 💡 OPTIMIZATION NOTES FOR THIS SPECIFIC VIDEO

### Why This Hook Works
- **Price anchor:** $15 creates immediate intrigue (how good can it be?)
- **Commitment phrase:** "never going back" → bold claim = keeps watching
- **Relatable:** Most viewers have bought bad cheap makeup before

### Why This Product Works
- **Universal need:** Everyone wears foundation (broad appeal)
- **Price point:** $15 = impulse buy range (low barrier to purchase)
- **Specific benefit:** "doesn't oxidize" = shows real knowledge, not generic review
- **Proof built-in:** "wearing it now since 7am" = visual evidence in frame

### Trust-Building Elements
- **Caveat included:** Pump is stiff (realistic, not glossy ad)
- **Casual tone:** "I don't even care" (friend vibe, not influencer)
- **Specific details:** "Medium Beige", "SPF 15", "seven a.m." (proves she actually uses it)

### Potential Weak Points
- **If low CTR:** CTA is passive ("link in bio"), consider stronger ask like "comment SHOP and I'll send the link"
- **If low saves:** Add more specific detail in Product Moment (e.g., "works for oily skin, combo skin, doesn't cling to dry patches")
- **If low shares:** Hook could be more polarizing (e.g., "This $15 foundation is better than Charlotte Tilbury")
